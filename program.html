Bridge Program

The ABC Bridge will follow a one-day schedule designed to balance Education, Collaboration, and Outreach. This structure ensures that participants gain shared foundations, work together to develop new insights and guidelines, and amplify the impact to the broader AAAI community.

Schedule

  • 09:00 – 12:30 — Morning (Education)
    Tutorials + case lab
  • 13:30 – 16:45 — Afternoon (Collaboration)
    Paper & poster session, debate, breakout groups
  • 16:45 – 17:30 — Closing (Outreach)
    Plenary summary & motivational talk

Program Elements

Education: Provides shared foundations through tutorials and case labs.
Collaboration: Drives new insights and joint guidelines via interactive sessions.
Outreach: Amplifies the impact of the bridge to the broader AAAI community.
